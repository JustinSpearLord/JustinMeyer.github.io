Robot Rebellion

Created in Unity using C#, Robot Rebellion is a top down shooter where the robots created in a factory break out and attack the workers. The player needs to destroy the robots and save his fellow workers.

Software and languages Used

  • Unity
  • C#
  • Blender

Premise

You play as a factory worker from a top down view who needs to destroy the enemy robots that periodically spawn and roam the area until they detect the player. You will also need to save your fellow workers that are frantically running around. When you compleate the first level you will then need to defeat the Boss in the final level.

Responsibilities

For this project I was responsible for creating everything in the game, excluding sound design. This all includes the custom rigged modles and animations, multiple levels, the Main Menu GUI, the player character and their controls, the projectile and damage, health and healthbars for all the characters, the enemy characters including the boss, and the spawners.

Spotlights

Custom Characters: I recived the robot mesh as an asset for this project so I took it into Blender added arms and matarials then rigged character to give it custom aniamtions. I also created the player from scratch, modeling and rigging it with custom materials and animations.

Boss Enemy: The boss character was an custom enemy type that had unique stats and once destroyed turned into a bunch of little faster enemies.
