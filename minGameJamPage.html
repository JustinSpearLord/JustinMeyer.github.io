Mini Games Jam

Shawnee State University hosted a game jam with the theme being mini-games. Working in a team of two and using Unreal Engine we created a medieval tournament style local verses game.

Software Used

  • Unreal Engine 4
  • Blueprints visual scripting

Premise

My partner and I had to create a game with the theme of mini games in the alloted time of 48 hours. We decided to use Unreal Engine 4 for its capaciaty to itterate quickly and allowed us to get a basic playable game very quickly. We decided on using some of the free assets Epic Games had released and use them to create a medieval tournament styled game where two player can verse eachother in short tourney mini-games. Both players would use the same keyboard, one uses the left half and the other using the right half.

Responsibilities

In this colaboration project I worked on most of the two mini-games as well as the transitional level where the two opposing players can taunts eachother before a game starts.

Mini-Games

Jousting: In this mini-game we had a slider that would move up and down at varying speed and if you time your attack correctly and your opponent misses then you will execute a successful attack. If both players miss or there is a tie then the attacks would cancel out.

Rock, Paper, Sissors Dual: Here each player took alternating turns attacking and defending. The attacker could chose from three diffrent attacking swings and the defender had to match the correct defence for the attack.

Transitional level:We also added a fun transition level where the players can taunt eachother before they enter into a mini-game.
